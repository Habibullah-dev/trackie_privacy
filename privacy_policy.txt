Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Trackie ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our reading tracking application ("the App").

Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

1. User-Provided Information:
• Reading history and progress
• Book notes and quotes
• Reading goals and tasks
• User preferences and settings

2. Automatically Collected Information:
• Device information
• App usage statistics
• Reading session duration
• Performance and crash data

App Permissions
Our app requires the following permissions:

• RECORD_AUDIO: This permission is used solely for the text-to-speech feature that reads book content aloud.
We do not record, store, or transmit any audio from your device.
The microphone access is only used by the system text-to-speech engine to enable audio playback.

How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
• Provide and improve the App's features
• Track your reading progress
• Generate reading insights and statistics
• Maintain and enhance app performance
• Send important notifications about your reading goals
• Provide personalized book recommendations

Data Storage and Security
• Your data is stored securely on your device and in our cloud servers
• We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
• We regularly backup your data to prevent loss
• You can export your data at any time
• We never sell your personal information to third parties

Your Rights
You have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Export your data
• Delete your account and associated data
• Opt-out of analytics collection
• Request data correction
• Control app permissions

Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services for:
• Analytics and crash reporting
• Book information databases
• Cloud storage
• Push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

Children Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or via email.
